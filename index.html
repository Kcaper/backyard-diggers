Not your average sandpit

A proper dig site, built for little hands

Backyard Diggers started with a few small machines and a sandpit. The problem? The little ones couldn't dig deep or move real loads. So we went bigger — full-function RC excavators and tipper trucks that actually scoop, swing, haul and dump.

The sandpit became a poured-concrete course with ramps, levels and proper construction theming — hazard signs, hi-vis, the lot. Every week there's a new section, a fresh challenge and more terrain to conquer. It's the kind of place that looks even better in person than it does on a screen.

What your little operator will do

Gear up, take the controls and put in a full day's work — no two visits dig out quite the same.

Drive Real Diggers

Take command of full-function remote-control excavators that genuinely scoop, swing and dump — just like the big yellow machines on a real site.

Load, Haul & Dump

A fleet of tipper trucks and front-loaders to fill, carry and tip. Move the "rubble" across the site and back the truck up to the pile.

Conquer the Dig Site

Ramps, raised levels, tunnels and a winding poured track. Steer up, over and through the multi-level course without tipping your load.

Beat the Record

Think you've got the fastest hands on site? Race the circuit and try to top the Backyard Diggers leaderboard. Parents-vs-kids encouraged.

Micro Machines

A dedicated zone of pocket-sized machines for the littlest builders — perfect for younger siblings and tiny first-timers.

Gear Up

Hard hats and hi-vis vests waiting at the gate. Every visitor signs on as site foreman for the day — dress-up is half the fun.

Do I need to book? +

Yes — message us on Facebook or WhatsApp to grab a slot. Booking keeps things calm and makes sure every child gets proper machine time.

How much does it cost? +

R150 per person for the hour, with free chips & juice for the kids included. Birthday parties are quoted to suit your group.

What ages is it for? +

The sweet spot is roughly 4 to 8 years old, but older kids — and plenty of dads — get just as hooked once they're behind the controls.

What if it rains? +

No problem. The play area is fully undercover and shaded, so a bit of weather won't stop the dig.

Can we host a birthday party? +

Absolutely — it's our specialty. We keep parties small and exclusive. Message us and we'll put together a package for your group.

What should we wear? +

Comfy clothes that can handle a little sand. We supply the hard hats and hi-vis vests on arrival.
