The story behind the dig site

It started with a couple of machines in the backyard

It really did start in our backyard. I picked up a couple of RC machines just for fun, and whenever family and friends came round I'd bring them out to play. That's when it clicked — it doesn't matter who picks up the controls, everyone has a jol. Kids, parents, grannies, mates… nobody wanted to put them down.

So we went all in and built a proper little world for it — and it's very much a family affair. My mom and my nephew are part of the crew at our play sessions, helping settle the little ones in, keeping an eye on everyone and making sure every child feels welcome and looked after. There's always a warm, friendly face on hand.

Yes, I work in construction, so the theming and the hand-built ramps, levels and tracks come naturally (the kids love helping build them too). But at heart this is a safe, supervised, family-run space — a secure venue with caring hands on deck. At the end of the day it's about one simple thing: happy, busy kids and parents who can finally relax.

What your little operator will do

Drive Real Diggers

Take command of full-function remote-control excavators that genuinely scoop, swing and dump — just like the big yellow machines on a real site.

A fleet of tipper trucks and front-loaders to fill, carry and tip. Move the "rubble" across the site and back the truck up to the pile.

Ramps, raised levels, tunnels and a winding poured track. Steer up, over and through the multi-level course without tipping your load.

Think you've got the fastest hands on site? Race the circuit and try to top the Backyard Diggers leaderboard. Parents-vs-kids encouraged.

Micro Machines

Do I need to book? +

Yes — use the booking form below, or WhatsApp us on 073 257 1112 to grab a slot. Booking keeps things calm and makes sure every child gets proper machine time.

How much does it cost? +

R150 per child for the hour, with free chips & juice for the kids. Parents play along free when they're here with their kids — so moms and dads can jump on the controls at no charge. Hard hats and hi-vis are provided to wear on site. Birthday parties are quoted to suit your group, and adult-only outings (team building & corporate events) are priced per group — just ask.

When are you open? +

Monday to Friday 9am–7pm, and Saturdays, Sundays & public holidays 7am–9pm. Sessions run by booking, so message us to lock in your time.

What ages is it for? +

Backyard Diggers is built for kids aged 4 and up, with no upper limit. Little ones love the micro machines, bigger kids take on the full multi-level course — and plenty of moms and dads get just as hooked once they're behind the controls.

What if it rains? +

No problem. The play area is fully undercover and shaded, so a bit of weather won't stop the dig — it's a solid rainy-day, weekend or school-holiday activity whatever the forecast says.

Can we host a birthday party? +

Absolutely — it's our specialty. We keep parties small and exclusive. Message us and we'll put together a package for your group.

Who is Backyard Diggers good for? +

Everyone from toddlers to grandparents, honestly. It's a favourite for sibling and cousin day outs, mommy-and-me or daddy-and-me mornings, and grandparent visits — we regularly see grannies and grandpas on the controls right alongside the kids. Homeschool pods, crèche and nursery school groups are welcome too — just message us to arrange a group time.

Do you do schools, team building or events? +

Yes! We offer play-session tickets as school raffle prizes, run hands-on team-building challenges for companies, and make a great stop on a treasure hunt or city-wide challenge. We're also a popular pick for homeschool groups, crèche outings and school-holiday programmes. Message us and we'll tailor something to suit.

Do you only cater to Weltevreden Park? +

Not at all — we're based in Weltevreden Park, but families regularly make the trip from across Roodepoort, Randburg and Johannesburg for a day out. Easy to find, with parking on site.

What should we wear? +

Comfy clothes that can handle a little sand. We provide hard hats and hi-vis vests to wear on site — they stay with us at the end, ready for the next little operator.
